Banks deploying AI face a choice between three model architectures, each with distinct implications for capability, cost, governance, and overhead. For workflows that span multiple sensitivity levels within a single session, the right answer may be a hybrid pipeline where different tiers are called in sequence. This framework evaluates your use case across nine dimensions and recommends the right tier or pipeline.

Pick your domain, answer nine questions across three sections, score where the bank is today (not where it hopes to be), and click "See result." Or start below with the pressure test scenarios — real banking workflows pre-loaded and ready to explore.

FrontierFrontier modelMaximum reasoning depth and security awareness. Required when the stakes of a wrong output are high.
HybridHybrid pipelineMultiple tiers in sequence. A balance query handled by an SLM escalates mid-conversation to the frontier model for consumer rights advice.
